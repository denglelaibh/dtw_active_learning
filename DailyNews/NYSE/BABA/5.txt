The stock's current Portfolio Grader score places it 1 among the 131 companies in this industry group, positioning it among the leaders; in the top decile of the sector with a ranking of 3 among the 706 companies in the sector, and number 12 in the nearly 5,000 company Portfolio Grader universe.Portfolio Grader currently ranks BABA as a Strong Buy.
