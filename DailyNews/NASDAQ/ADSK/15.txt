         News provided byNov 01, 2017, 08:00 ET Share this articleSAN RAFAEL, Calif., Nov. 1, 2017 /PRNewswire/ -- Autodesk, Inc. (NASDAQ:    ADSK) today announced that it will broadcast its third quarter fiscal 2018 financial results conference call live via its website Tuesday,  November 28, 2017 at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time. Autodesk will host a live webcast call at www.autodesk.com/investors. An audio replay webcast and podcast will also be available after 5:00 p.m. Pacific Time on our website at www.autodesk.com/investors.
