(RTTNews.com) - The Priceline Group Inc. ( PCLN ) announced a profit for its third quarter that increased compared to the same period last year.The company said its bottom line climbed to $1.76 billion, or $35.22 per share. Analysts' estimates typically exclude special items.The company said revenue for the quarter rose 20.1% to $4.43 billion.
