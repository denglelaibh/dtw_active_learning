Human-resources software firm Automatic Data Processing Inc., locked in a proxy battle with William Ackman, says it will file a complaint with the Securities and Exchange Commission over what it calls “false and misleading” claims the activist investor made this week.ADP said in a statement Tuesday it “strongly rejects” statements made by Mr. Ackman, founder of Pershing Square Capital Management, referring to an 18-page letter he sent to proxy adviser Institutional Shareholder Services on Monday.
